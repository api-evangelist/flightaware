In previous posts, we explored the technology powering the FlightAware data platform —the global terrestrial ADS-B network , the FlightAware Firehose℠ feed streaming millions of near real-time flight events, and the machine learning models that generate industry-leading predictive analytics. While those are the technical components, this blog focuses on the operational architecture: how data intelligence is integrated into the mission-critical systems of sophisticated global aviation organizations. FlightAware data is an active intelligence layer.
