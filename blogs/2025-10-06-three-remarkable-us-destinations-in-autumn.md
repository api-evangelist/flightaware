<p>From the rocky coastline of Maine to the sandy shores of southern California, the vast landscape of the United States features a myriad of spectacular destinations. During the autumn months, warm colors that fill the forests surrounding breathtaking settings are particularly vibrant. In the aviation world, it is recognized that fall sights are best viewed from the sky. This post will highlight three remarkable autumn destinations. Each destination is located in a unique region of the country and offers an abundance of conveniences and pastimes upon arrival.</p>
